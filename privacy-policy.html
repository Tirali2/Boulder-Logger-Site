Boulder Logger - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

Information We Collect

Boulder Logger is designed to help users track their bouldering progress. The app collects the following information:

  • User-created climbing place configurations
  • Climbing session data including dates, attempts, and grades
  • Google account information (only when you choose to use the backup functionality)

How We Use Your Information

The data collected by Boulder Logger is used solely for:

  • Providing the core functionality of tracking and analyzing your climbing progress
  • Enabling cloud backup of your data using Google Drive (only when explicitly initiated by you)

Google Drive API Usage

Boulder Logger uses the Google Drive API to provide backup and restore functionality. When you choose to use this feature:

  • We request permission to create and manage files in your Google Drive
  • We only create/update a single backup file in your Drive
  • We do not access any other files in your Google Drive
  • The backup file contains only your climbing data from the app
  • Google authentication tokens are stored locally on your device only

Data Storage

Your climbing data is primarily stored locally on your device. When you use the backup feature, a copy of this data is stored in your personal Google Drive account.

Third-Party Services

Boulder Logger uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication with Google Drive
  • Google Drive API: For cloud backup functionality

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
